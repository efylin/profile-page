The Truman Show

I watched this for High School English and it was one of the only English texts I actually enjoyed. The film has a unique concept and is engaging from start to end. Additionally, the visuals are so nice and pleasing to the eyes. To wrap it all up, despite this film being made decades ago, the theme of media is highly relevant in this day and age!

Parasite

Coming into this film, I had low expectations since my friend's summary of the film sounded absolutely ridiculous. Nonetheless, the 'ridiculous' plot completely exceeded my expectations. This film is very well executed with incredible acting, music and most importantly, cinematography. Very interesting theme of class inequality explored well.

Mamma Mia!

This one's more of a comfort movie for me. There's a strong sense of aesthetic in this movie which I really liked to see. Although everything in the plot falls into place a bit too perfectly, I wasn't too bothered by this because I appreciated the film for the good vibes. All the ABBA songs are also really fun to sing to and blend in well with the scenes.
